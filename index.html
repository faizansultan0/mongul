About Mogul Entrepreneurs Development Center

I see the importance of small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in developing the economy of Lahore. Hence, I have decided to establish Mogul Entrepreneurs Development Center in 2007 to lead the march to support and develop entrepreneurs, and encourage the youth to innovate and to establish enterprises

From this supreme vision, the establishment will be able to support a considerable number of young entrepreneurs to start their enterprises. In addition to its primary role in promoting the culture of entrepreneurship amongst youth & to develop their leadership potential
